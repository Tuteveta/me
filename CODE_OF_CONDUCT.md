# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The DICT M&E Dashboard is developed in service of the Department of Information Communication & Technology (DICT), Government of Papua New Guinea. Everyone involved in this project — contributors, maintainers, and users — is expected to act professionally and respectfully.

## Standards

**Expected behaviour:**

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing technical viewpoints and approaches.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the dashboard and its users — DICT staff and the people of Papua New Guinea.
- Show consideration for government data sensitivity and privacy obligations.

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing or sharing private or sensitive government data without authorisation.
- Conduct that would embarrass DICT or the Government of Papua New Guinea.
- Any behaviour that violates applicable Papua New Guinea law or government IT policy.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and any communication related to the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the DICT ICT team. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
